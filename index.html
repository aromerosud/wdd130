Juli

Juli is a city located in the Puno region, on the shores of Lake Titicaca. Known as the “Little Rome of America” for the richness of its colonial temples, it combines history, tradition, and unique natural landscapes. It is an important cultural and religious destination that reflects both the Andean legacy and Spanish influence.
